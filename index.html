Our Services

Comprehensive Testing Solutions

We provide a full range of pre-completion testing services to ensure your project meets all regulatory requirements.

Sound Insulation Testing

Pre-completion testing to Approved Document Part E of the Building Regulations. We test airborne and impact sound insulation between dwellings to ensure compliance.

  • Airborne sound testing (walls & floors)
  • Impact sound testing (floors)
  • New-build & conversions
  • Pre-completion certification
Get a Quote

Air Tightness Testing

Air permeability testing to Approved Document Part L of the Building Regulations. We measure the air leakage rate of your building envelope using blower door equipment.

  • Domestic & commercial properties
  • Blower door testing
  • Part L1A & Part L2A compliance
  • EPC-ready reporting
